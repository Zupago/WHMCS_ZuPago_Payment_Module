To install Perfect Money payment method in your WHMCS follow these instructions:

1. Upload contents of "upload" folder to your web-server to folder where WHMCS is installed.

2. Go to WHMCS Admin Panel > Setup > Payments > Payment Gateways and activate ZuPago HyBrid (HD) Wallet module.

3. Enter all required fields as described there to finish setting up of ZuPago HyBrid (HD) Wallet payment method.

4. For any issue https://zupago.pe/contact-us

ZuPago WHMCS Modules Now Support Bitcoin Cash (BCC/BCH)
